Membership Level

Membership Level Benefits

NP Membership (Non Profit Organization)

Annual membership price: This level of membership is not priced as it corresponds to a non-profit organization.

Benefits of this Membership:

  • Access to networking events.
  • Inclusion in online directories of the Chamber of Commerce.

Bronze Membership

Annual membership price: $400

Benefits of this Membership:

  • Benefits of previous membership.
  • Low prices to participate in seminars and trainings for entrepreneurs.
  • Access to chamber news and business updates in the area.
  • Recommendations from the chamber's team to improve the business or company once a month.

Silver Membership

Annual membership price: $750

Benefits of this Membership:

  • Benefits of previous membership.
  • Free access to events with national and international businessmen.
  • Free participation in marketing and business development seminars and trainings, etc.
  • Free advertising on the Chamber of Commerce website, once a month.
  • 10% discount for the following year's membership.

Gold Membership

Annual membership price: $1200

Benefits of this Membership:

  • Benefits of previous membership.
  • 15% discount for the following year's membership.
  • VIP access to meetings and seminars with local and international businessmen, as well as government representatives.
  • Free and prominent advertising on the Chamber of Commerce's website, every month.
  • Personalized advice from the Chamber of Commerce team in weekly meetings with other Gold members.
  • Exclusive participation in national events with entrepreneurs from other regions.
  • Direct participation in decisions to improve the Chamber of Commerce in the local area and to implement plans to support smaller businesses.
