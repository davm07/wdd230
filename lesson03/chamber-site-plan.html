Site Name:

Lommel Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

This name represents the city on which the chamber of commerce operates

Site Purpose:

The two main purposes of the Lommel Chamber of Commerce are:

  1. It serves as a networking platform for local businesses and community members helping them increase business opportunities in the area.
  2. The chamber promotes activities, events and festivals that generate a positive impact on the local economy.

Scenarios:

  • Where can I find information to start my business in the local area?
  • Where can I find job openings in the local businesses?
  • How can I get in contact with a determined business or company in the local area?
